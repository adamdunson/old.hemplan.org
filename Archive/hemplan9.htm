HempLAN 9.0 - Un sueño de un Mexicano

¿Cuándo?: El sábado 22.12.2007 en 14:00 hasta el domingo 23.12.2007

Important Pre-LAN:

  • IT staff is there for your convenience, however they are not paid so try to keep everything in working order yourself.
  • Please install a CD-image mounting program such as Daemon Tools as many of the games we play will be distributed as an image of the disc (such as .iso or .mdf/mds).
  • It would really be better for all of us if you would install anti-virus software. Anti-spyware/-adware software would be great as well. Below is a list of free (zero-cost) programs
    • Grisoft's free anti-virus/-malware downloads
    • Ad-Aware Free
    • Spybot: S&D

Main Games:

  • PC
    • TrackMania United (contact Zyzy)
    • Warsow
    • LAHt BIKes (Armagetron)
    • Team Fortress 2 (Steam)
    • Day of Defeat: Source (Steam)
    • Counter-Strike: Source (Steam)
  • Wii/GameCube
    • WiiSports
    • Super Smash Brothers Melee
    • Super Mario Strikers
    • WarioWare: Smooth Moves
  • Other Games:
    • GTA: SA Multiplayer Mod
    • GRAW/GRAW 2
    • <insert half-life 2 mod here> (Steam)
    • Warcraft III DotA
    • Starcraft

You need Steam and related games if you want to play them, everything else will be provided at the LAN.

Most important stuff to bring:

  • Computer, keyboard, mouse (& mousepad)
  • $10 fee: $5 goes to parents for utilities, $5 goes towards funding the LAN party itself.
  • Powerstrip/UPS (If you don't bring a powerstrip, we can't promise access to outlets)
  • Food, drinks, and money (other than fee money). We won't be providing food or drink (unless there is leftover money), so expect to buy something or just bring your own (water is provided via the tap :P).

Less important stuff to bring:

  • Angry Mexican coolant.
  • Ethernet cables, just in case ours aren't working. If Zyzy has to make one, you pay $5 rental cable fee.
  • Headset or Headphones and a microphone. Absolutely no speakers (noise OMG).
  • A fan, especially if you have an awesome fan like Jordan's, unless you want to sweat like an angry Mexican.
  • Small folding tables that can hold a computer (just in case we need an extra).
  • A computer chair is optional but recommended for an angry Mexican.
  • GCN controllers and Wii controllers, if you have them. If you have one (and a Wii), create your Mii and save it to a controller so you can copy yours over as 'traveling' on the Wii(s).
  • Pillows and sheets, or sleeping bag, for sleeping like an angry Mexican after drinking plenty of cerveza.

Other Optional Costs:

  • Bring money for food. You can expect that someone will make a run to a food place at some point.
  • Zyzy is taking orders for Bawls (probably 16oz cans). The cost is $2.00 a-piece. Contact him online if you want him to get you some.
  • Zyzy can make you custom length ethernet cords if you want one to keep. The price depends on the length, but it's significantly cheaper than in a store.
  • For every $50 donated, you get a free chunk of out-dated computer hardware (like a 56k modem or an ISA-interface LAN card).

Other Notes:

  • Please take time to make an account on our Forum, if you haven't already. This is the best way to discuss LAN dates and games. You don't have to watch it daily, but this way we can discuss LAN stuff as a group.
  • If you have any suggestions for games for this or future LANs, post them on the forum and we will add them to the list.
  • GLHF eat babies (but not really 'cause that would be terrible)
